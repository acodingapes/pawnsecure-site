About PawnSecure SA

PawnSecure SA is South Africa’ s first digital-first pawn and buyback platform.

PawnSecure SA provides South Africa with a digital-first platform to sell valuables safely, with the added option of buying them back later. We are registered under the Second-Hand Goods Act and operate with full transparency, giving customers confidence in every transaction.

Our commitment:

  • ✅ Licensed & Compliant - Registered with SAPS under the Second-Hand Goods Act.
  • ✅ Transparency - Clear agreements with no hidden costs.
  • ✅ Secure Handling - Professional storage and insured protection of items.
